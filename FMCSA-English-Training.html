拦检情景对话（Officer – Driver）

英文中文
1. Can I have your CDL, registration, insurance, and medical card, please?请出示你的商业驾照、车辆注册、保险、健康证。
2. Can you show me your ELD or logbook?请出示你的电子记录设备或驾驶日志。
3. What are you hauling, and where are you headed?你在运什么货物，目的地是哪里？
4. Where did you come from today?今天你从哪里出发？
5. How long have you been driving today?今天你开了多久？
6. Have you had your break/rest time today?你今天有按规定休息吗？
7. How much does your load weigh?你的货物总重是多少？
8. Are you carrying any hazardous materials?有任何危险品吗？
9. Can I have your bill of lading?请出示提货单。
10. Did you do pre-trip inspection today?你今天做了行车前车检了吗？
11. I’ll need to inspect the vehicle. Can you step out?我需要检查车辆，请下车。
12. I’m going to have you perform level II inspection.请做二级车检。
13. Everything checks out, but one of your brake lights is out. You’ll need to fix that before you continue.检查都合格，但你的一个刹车灯坏了，需要修好才能继续行驶。
14. I’m issuing a citation for the brake light.我会因为刹车灯问题开一张罚单。
15. Alright, drive safely and have a good day.好的，安全驾驶，祝你一天愉快。
